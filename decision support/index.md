# Role-Based Decision Support Prompts

## QA (Quality Assurance)

1. "Help decide whether [release] is ready to ship by weighing test results, open defects, business urgency, and rollback options."
2. "Create a decision matrix for choosing between manual, automated, and exploratory testing for [feature]."
3. "Evaluate whether to block release for these defects [defects], using severity, likelihood, customer impact, and workaround quality."
4. "Compare test management tools for [team/context] using criteria for cost, usability, integrations, reporting, and maintenance."
5. "Help prioritize QA work for [release] under time constraints using risk, coverage gaps, and stakeholder impact."
6. "Create a trade-off analysis for investing in test automation versus improving test data and environment reliability."
7. "Recommend whether to expand UAT for [feature], considering user impact, complexity, confidence, and schedule pressure."
8. "Build a framework for deciding which regression tests should be automated first."
9. "Evaluate options for handling flaky tests in CI, including quarantine, fix-first policy, retries, and ownership rules."
10. "Recommend a QA process change for [problem], explaining decision criteria, risks, and expected outcomes."

## Business Analysts

1. "Create a decision matrix for choosing between solution options for [business problem]."
2. "Help decide whether [requirement/change] should be in scope by weighing business value, urgency, effort, dependencies, and risk."
3. "Compare build, buy, and process-change options for [initiative] using a structured trade-off framework."
4. "Recommend a delivery order for these capabilities [capabilities] based on dependencies, value, risk, and stakeholder readiness."
5. "Create a decision brief for [stakeholder decision], including context, options, pros, cons, recommendation, and next steps."
6. "Evaluate whether to proceed with [project] based on evidence, assumptions, constraints, and unresolved questions."
7. "Build a prioritization framework for requirements in [project], including must-have, should-have, could-have, and out-of-scope logic."
8. "Help resolve conflicting stakeholder needs around [issue] by identifying trade-offs and decision criteria."
9. "Compare process redesign versus system enhancement for [workflow problem]."
10. "Recommend which business metrics should guide the decision on [initiative], explaining why each metric matters."

## Developers

1. "Create a decision matrix for implementation options for [feature], comparing complexity, maintainability, performance, security, and timeline."
2. "Help decide whether to refactor or rewrite [module] based on risk, cost, test coverage, defects, and future roadmap."
3. "Compare libraries or frameworks for [task] and recommend one using explicit criteria."
4. "Evaluate whether to add [technical dependency] by weighing benefits, maintenance burden, security, and ecosystem maturity."
5. "Recommend a database design for [use case], comparing schema options and query patterns."
6. "Help decide whether [bug] needs a hotfix, normal release fix, workaround, or monitoring only."
7. "Create a trade-off analysis for synchronous versus asynchronous processing in [workflow]."
8. "Recommend a rollout strategy for [change], including feature flags, canary, monitoring, and rollback."
9. "Evaluate whether to optimize [performance issue] now or defer it, using user impact, evidence, cost, and risk."
10. "Create a framework for choosing what tests to add for [feature] under limited time."

## Senior Developers

1. "Create an architecture decision record for [decision], including context, options, decision criteria, recommendation, and consequences."
2. "Compare architectural options for [system] using scalability, reliability, cost, team ownership, migration path, and operational complexity."
3. "Help decide whether to adopt [technology] by weighing strategic fit, maturity, team readiness, ecosystem, risk, and exit cost."
4. "Create a trade-off analysis for monolith, modular monolith, microservices, and event-driven approaches for [context]."
5. "Recommend a migration strategy for [system] with criteria for risk, reversibility, delivery continuity, and user impact."
6. "Evaluate whether to pause feature work for technical debt reduction in [area], using business and engineering evidence."
7. "Build a decision framework for prioritizing platform investments across reliability, security, productivity, and cost reduction."
8. "Help resolve a cross-team technical disagreement about [decision] by identifying decision rights, criteria, options, and risks."
9. "Recommend an incident prevention investment for [system] based on likelihood, impact, cost, and detection gaps."
10. "Create a decision memo for [technical initiative] that leadership can approve or reject."

## Product People

1. "Create a decision matrix for prioritizing these roadmap items [items] using user value, business value, confidence, effort, and risk."
2. "Help decide whether to build [feature] now, later, or not at all using evidence, opportunity cost, and strategic fit."
3. "Compare target customer segments for [product] and recommend where to focus first."
4. "Evaluate whether to launch [feature] broadly, beta it, delay it, or cancel it based on readiness and risk."
5. "Create a trade-off analysis for solving [problem] with UX changes, workflow changes, automation, or support content."
6. "Recommend success metrics for [initiative], explaining which should drive decisions and which should be guardrails."
7. "Help decide between competing product hypotheses [hypotheses] by identifying evidence, experiment cost, and upside."
8. "Create a prioritization framework for customer feedback from [source]."
9. "Evaluate pricing or packaging options for [product], considering customer value, willingness to pay, complexity, and sales motion."
10. "Create a product decision memo for [decision], including context, options, recommendation, risks, and next evidence needed."
