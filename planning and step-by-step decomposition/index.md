# Role-Based Planning and Step-by-Step Decomposition Prompts

## QA (Quality Assurance)

1. "Break down testing for [feature] into a sequenced test plan covering unit, integration, regression, and UAT phases with entry/exit criteria for each."
2. "Given this user story: [story], generate a complete checklist of positive, negative, edge case, and boundary test scenarios."
3. "Create a step-by-step regression testing schedule for our [release], prioritizing test cases by risk and historical defect density."
4. "Decompose the goal 'achieve 80% automated test coverage on the checkout module' into a 12-week plan with weekly milestones."
5. "Build a bug triage workflow checklist from initial report through verification, including severity criteria and escalation paths."
6. "Outline the steps to set up a new test environment for [system], including data seeding, configuration, access provisioning, and smoke validation."
7. "Turn this requirements doc [doc] into a traceability matrix mapping each requirement to specific test cases."
8. "Create a release readiness checklist covering test completion, defect thresholds, performance benchmarks, and sign-off criteria."
9. "Plan a sequenced approach to introducing API contract testing into our existing manual test suite over one quarter."
10. "Decompose 'reduce production defects by 40% in 6 months' into specific QA process changes, training needs, and tooling investments."

## Business Analysts

1. "Break down the discovery phase for [project] into stakeholder interviews, document reviews, and workshop sessions with a 3-week timeline."
2. "Turn this business goal: [goal] into a sequenced list of capabilities required, dependencies between them, and a recommended delivery order."
3. "Create a checklist for documenting an end-to-end business process, including current state, pain points, future state, and gap analysis."
4. "Decompose 'migrate from legacy CRM to Salesforce' into workstreams covering data, process, integration, training, and change management."
5. "Generate a step-by-step requirements elicitation plan for [stakeholder group], including question sets, artifacts to produce, and validation steps."
6. "Build a checklist for writing a high-quality user story including acceptance criteria, dependencies, NFRs, and definition of ready."
7. "Plan the steps to facilitate a process mapping workshop, from pre-work through follow-up deliverables."
8. "Decompose 'improve onboarding conversion by 15%' into hypotheses to test, data needed, and a sequenced experimentation roadmap."
9. "Create a stakeholder analysis and engagement plan checklist for [project], covering RACI, communication cadence, and decision rights."
10. "Turn this RFP response goal into a project plan with research, drafting, review, and submission milestones over 4 weeks."

## Developers

1. "Break down implementing [feature] into tasks ordered by dependency, with estimated effort and clear acceptance criteria for each."
2. "Generate a step-by-step plan to add [new endpoint] to our API, including schema design, validation, tests, docs, and deployment."
3. "Create a checklist for setting up a new microservice from scratch: repo, CI/CD, observability, health checks, and security baseline."
4. "Decompose this bug fix [bug] into investigation steps, reproduction, root cause analysis, fix, test, and rollout."
5. "Plan the sequenced steps to refactor [module] without breaking existing functionality, using a strangler pattern."
6. "Turn this Jira ticket [ticket] into a granular task list I can complete in a single day, with clear checkpoints."
7. "Build a pre-PR checklist covering tests, linting, docs, security review, performance considerations, and breaking-change assessment."
8. "Decompose 'add OAuth2 to our app' into a sequenced implementation plan covering provider setup, backend, frontend, session handling, and testing."
9. "Create a step-by-step debugging plan for an intermittent production issue where logs are sparse."
10. "Plan the steps to onboard myself to a new codebase: directory structure, build/run, key abstractions, data flow, and first contribution."

## Senior Developers

1. "Decompose 'migrate our monolith to event-driven architecture' into a phased roadmap with risk, dependencies, and rollback plans for each phase."
2. "Plan a sequenced technical debt reduction initiative over 2 quarters, balancing feature delivery with refactor priorities."
3. "Break down designing [system] into RFC sections: context, goals, non-goals, options considered, decision, risks, and migration plan."
4. "Create a checklist for conducting a thorough architecture review covering scalability, security, observability, cost, and operational readiness."
5. "Turn the goal 'reduce p99 latency from 800ms to 200ms' into an investigation plan, hypothesis-driven experiments, and rollout milestones."
6. "Decompose mentoring a mid-level engineer to senior into a 6-month plan with skills to develop, projects to own, and feedback cadence."
7. "Build a sequenced incident postmortem process from page through action item closure, including templates and review gates."
8. "Plan the steps to introduce [new technology, e.g., Kafka, gRPC] into our stack: POC, evaluation, pilot, rollout, and deprecation of the old approach."
9. "Decompose 'improve developer productivity by 25%' into measurable initiatives across tooling, process, and team structure."
10. "Create a step-by-step plan for leading a cross-team technical initiative, including alignment, RFC, execution, and post-launch review."

## Product People

1. "Decompose this product vision [vision] into a 12-month roadmap with quarterly themes, key outcomes, and major bets."
2. "Turn 'launch [feature] in Q3' into a sequenced launch plan covering discovery, design, build, beta, GA, and post-launch measurement."
3. "Break down a discovery sprint for [problem area] into user research, competitive analysis, hypothesis formation, and prioritization."
4. "Create a checklist for writing a strong PRD covering problem, users, success metrics, scope, NFRs, risks, and open questions."
5. "Decompose 'enter [new market segment]' into research, positioning, MVP definition, GTM, and success criteria milestones."
6. "Plan a sequenced experimentation roadmap to validate [hypothesis] with research, prototype, A/B test, and rollout decisions."
7. "Turn customer feedback from [source] into a prioritized backlog using an explicit scoring framework like RICE or WSJF."
8. "Build a launch readiness checklist covering product, engineering, support, marketing, sales, legal, and analytics."
9. "Decompose 'improve activation rate from X% to Y%' into a funnel analysis, hypothesis tree, and sequenced experiment plan."
10. "Create a step-by-step plan for running quarterly planning: input gathering, prioritization, roadmap drafting, alignment, and commitment."
