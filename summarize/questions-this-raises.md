## The Prompt

Read this content as a critical but constructive reviewer. Generate follow-up questions that a thoughtful reader would ask.

### Categories of Questions

**Clarification Questions**
- What's unclear or ambiguous?
- What terms or concepts need definition?
- Where is more detail needed?

**Challenge Questions**
- What assumptions are being made?
- What evidence supports the key claims?
- What counter-arguments aren't addressed?

**Gap Questions**
- What's missing that should be here?
- What scenarios or edge cases aren't covered?
- Who or what is affected but not mentioned?

**Implementation Questions**
- How would this actually work in practice?
- What could go wrong?
- What's the plan if assumptions prove wrong?

**Priority Questions**
- Why this approach over alternatives?
- Why now? What's the urgency?
- What happens if we don't do this?
