# Pros / Cons / Risks Analysis

Every proposal or decision has trade-offs that aren't always made explicit. This prompt forces a balanced analysis — what's good, what's bad, what could go wrong, and what to do about it. Useful for evaluating options, preparing for decision meetings, or stress-testing your own ideas before presenting them.

---

## The Prompt

Analyze this content and produce a structured evaluation of the proposal, approach, or decision described.

### Analysis Format

**One-Sentence Summary**
What is being proposed or decided?

**Pros (Advantages)**
- List each benefit
- Be specific — "faster" isn't helpful; "reduces build time from 10 min to 2 min" is

**Cons (Disadvantages)**
- List each drawback
- Include costs: time, money, complexity, learning curve

**Risks**
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| [What could go wrong] | Low/Med/High | Low/Med/High | [How to reduce or handle] |

**Assumptions**
- What must be true for this to work?
- What are we taking for granted?

**Unknowns**
- What don't we know that we should?
- What would change our analysis?

**Recommendation**
Based on this analysis:
- Proceed / Proceed with caution / Do not proceed / Need more information
- Key factors driving this recommendation
- What would change your mind?
