# Just Bullet Points

Sometimes you just need the facts, fast. This prompt strips content down to pure bullet points — no prose, no fluff, no narrative. Maximum information density for quick reference, note-taking, or when you need to extract the substance from verbose content.

---

## The Prompt

Convert this content into bullet points. Nothing else.

Rules:
- One idea per bullet
- No introductory text or transitions
- No paragraphs — bullets only
- Keep each bullet to one line when possible
- Use sub-bullets only when there's a clear parent-child relationship
- Preserve specific facts: names, numbers, dates, quotes
- Remove filler words and hedging language

Format:
```
- [Point]
- [Point]
  - [Sub-point if needed]
- [Point]
```

Do not include:
- Summary or introduction
- Concluding remarks
- Commentary or opinions (unless quoting the source)
- "The document says..." or similar framing
